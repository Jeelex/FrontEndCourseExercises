PERSON OF THE WEEK



Sara Soueidan (@SaraSoueidan) is an independent UI/design engineer, based in Lebanon. She works with companies and agencies around the world, building clean, responsive front-ends that are focused on accessibility, progressive enhancement, and performance. Committed to making the web platform better for everyone, Sara shares her knowledge as a speaker and author. She wrote the Codrops CSS Reference, co-authored Smashing Book 5, and was voted Developer of the Year in the 2015 net awards. Thank you for everything you do for the community, dear Sara!
